# Compost Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 29, 2025

## Overview
Compost is a browser extension that saves links temporarily. Your privacy is important to us.

## What data we collect
Compost stores only the links you save, along with timestamps. This data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.

## What we don't collect
- We do not collect personal information
- We do not track your browsing activity
- We do not transmit your saved links to any server
- We do not use analytics or tracking
- We do not sell or share any data

## Third-party services
Compost uses noembed.com to fetch page titles for saved links. Only the URL is sent to this service to retrieve the title. No personal data is shared.

## Data storage
All link data is stored locally on your device and never leaves your browser. Data automatically expires and is deleted after 30 days.

## Permissions used
- **clipboardRead**: Used to auto-paste URLs from your clipboard when you open the extension, for convenience. No clipboard data is stored or transmitted.
- **storage**: Used to save your links locally on your device.

## Contact
If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/mrslbt/compost).
